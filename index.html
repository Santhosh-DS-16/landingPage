Main Characters

Hades

The king of the underworld, with a heart of gold.

Persephone

The goddess of spring, with a hidden side.

Zeus

Zeus, a yellow-skinned man with long hair, is a chaotic and egotistical figure in Olympus known for his many relationships and representing power and corruption.

Poseidon

Poseidon, Zeus's green-skinned brother, is a carefree, loyal god with a quick temper and a polygamous relationship.

Eros

Eros, the maroon-skinned god of love, is a supportive friend to Persephone while navigating his own challenges, including his relationship with Psyche.
